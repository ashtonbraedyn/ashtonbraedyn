Instructions

Build towers and walls to claim territory and level up. Destroy your opponents and conquer the map!

 

Move: WASD

Build: Right Click or Space

Aim: Mouse cursor or arrow keys

Shoot: Left click or UP

 

Not sure what to do? Some tips to get started:

 

  1. Build Walls: Upgrade your copter's abilities by gaining XP and levelling up. You gain XP by building walls and controlling territory. 
  2. Create & Destroy: Earn points by controlling the map, busting walls, and blasting other copters. 
  3. Level Up: Reach level 20 to earn a special ability. Try a few and figure out which one you like best!
  4. Keep Going: When your copter is destroyed, you lose some XP and levels, but you keep most of your ability points.

Choose Your own Adventure

One day, there was a MONSTER APOCALYPSE! It was terrifying, and you were one of very few survivors in the small town.
You were starving a month after the Monster Apocalypse, so you decided to snatch some food from a store with your weapon, the Slicer.
You only knew the 2 closest stores; The Mall and the Market. You've seen just about every zombie movie, so you know alot.
Zombies LOVE the mall, so more inside the mall, and less in the way. The Market has a different story; less inside, more in the way.
Which way do you go?
